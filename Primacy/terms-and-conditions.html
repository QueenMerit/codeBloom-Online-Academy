🎓 Structure of Our Program

Classes run in batches. Each batch is divided into stages:

  • Stage 1–3 (Free Batch)
    • Stage 1: HTML Level 1 – free training and assessment.
    • Stage 2: HTML Level 2 – accessible to students who pass Stage 1.
    • Stage 3: CSS – unlocked by paying a small certificate fee, which serves as a commitment fee (not a class fee).
  • Stage 4 (Paid Batch)
    • JavaScript Class – NOT free. Students pay a discounted rate for both training and certification.
    • Non-codeBloom students pay the full price.

📜 Certificate Replacement Policy

If a certificate is lost or reported missing, and codeBloom confirms it was sent, a replacement certificate will incur a ₦2500 processing fee.

🧾 Participation and Re-runs

  • CSS/JavaScript students who miss their class will not be allowed to rerun it for free.
  • Rerunning requires paying the certificate fee again to join a new batch—even if the student previously received a certificate.
  • Students are strongly encouraged to take their assigned batch seriously.
