A Bit About the Founder

Amdalat Oladele, popularly known as QueenMerit, is the founder of codeBloom Online Academy. A passionate educator, tech enthusiast, and mentor, she has dedicated her journey to bridging the gap between aspiring developers and the tech industry. Her interest in coding sparked during her last year at the University of Ilorin, where she actively engaged in self-learning and mentoring others. After completing her NYSC, she focused on expanding her expertise in web development, digital skills, and affiliate marketing.

Her love for teaching and technology led to the creation of codeBloom Online Academy, a space where students can learn, grow, and turn their passion for coding into real-world opportunities.

About the Academy
